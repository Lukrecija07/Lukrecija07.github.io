My Family and Home

I grew up in a mixed household, my mother grew up speaking Russian and my father fully Lithuanian, however my first language was English.

I was born in Klaipeda, Lithuania but grew up in Manchester, England since the age of 2. I have a younger brother who was born in England and we communicate in a mix of Lithuanian and English. We now have 2 dogs, one yorkshire terrior called Pippa and a German Pincher named Macho.
